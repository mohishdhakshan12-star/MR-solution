ENGINEERING PROTOCOL

Our deterministic sequence of execution, ensuring physical safety, accuracy, and compliance at every node.

01
PHASE 1 // DIAGNOSIS

Concept & Feasibility

Comprehensive site study, electrical load profiling, and grid compliance analysis. We define absolute physical boundaries before tracing a single layout.

OUTPUT: Technical Feasibility Report
02
PHASE 2 // SCHEMATIC

Design & Simulation

Circuit topology selection, component rating math, multi-layer board routing, and SPICE transient/thermal simulations. Designs are stress-tested in simulation models first.

OUTPUT: CAD Layout & Simulation Data
03
PHASE 3 // MATERIALIZE

Build & Prototype

BOM procurement, bare board fabrication, assembly supervision, and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) lab bench validation. We bring theoretical circuits into the physical domain.

OUTPUT: Verified Physical Prototypes
04
PHASE 4 // INTEGRATE

Deploy & Commission

On-site industrial installation oversight, switchgear wiring inspection, custom commissioning tests, and safety compliance handoff. Final voltage certification.

OUTPUT: Operating Facility Sign-off
